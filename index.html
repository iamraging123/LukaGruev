KROCKET V1

actively controlled rocket · 2026 (WIP)

A rocket that actively stabilizes itself in flight. Two-PCB avionics, a Kalman filter, custom fin actuators — every part designed and built from scratch.

It runs on an STM32F7 with barometers, IMUs, GNSS, magnetometers, and LoRa telemetry. PID gains were tuned in MATLAB against a Simulink 6-DOF model and cross-checked with CFD before any flight hardware was cut.

LUKSER

a ground-up 3D printer · 2023

After printing on an Ender 3 and SV06+, I designed a printer of my own — fast and reliable from the ground up. Every choice, from the frame to the hotend, was mine to make.

Seven independent motors. A custom-machined hotend pushing volumetric flow up to ~100 mm³/s, roughly 8× a stock Ender 3. The frame is 1.5" aluminum extrusion, ~30 lbs, dead solid.

RGSOUND

sound-reactive LED controller · 2025

Off-the-shelf LED controllers were too limited, too bulky, or too expensive. So I built my own — clean, custom, and exactly the right shape for my desk.

Custom KiCad PCB. ESP32 with onboard antenna, driving WS2812B addressables and reacting to audio in real time. The layout went through a few passes to land on a two-layer board to keep cost down. Running daily on my desk.
