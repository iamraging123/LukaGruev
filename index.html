KROCKET V1

Actively Controlled Rocket · 2026 (WIP)

After my earlier rockets, I wanted to push further and build one that actively stabilizes itself in flight. Every part from the two-PCB avionics stack to the Kalman filter to the custom fin actuators was designed and built from scratch.

It runs on an STM32F7 with barometers, IMUs, GNSS, magnetometers, and LoRa telemetry. I tuned the PID gains in MATLAB against a Simulink 6-DOF model cross-checked with CFD before any flight hardware was cut.

LUKSER

A Ground-Up 3D Printer · 2023

After printing on an Ender 3 and SV06+, I wanted to design a printer of my own one that was fast and reliable from the ground up. Every choice from the frame to the hotend was mine to make.

The motion system uses seven independent motors (most printers use three). The hotend is custom-machined with high-wattage nichrome wiring, pushing volumetric flow up to 100 mm³/s roughly 8× a stock Ender 3. The frame is 1.5" aluminum extrusion, ~30 lbs, dead solid.

RGSOUND

Custom LED Controller, Reactive to Sound · 2025

I built RGSOUND because I wanted a clean, custom way to drive the LED strips on my desk every off-the-shelf controller I tried was either too limited, too bulky, or too expensive.

Custom PCB designed from scratch in KiCad. ESP32 with onboard antenna, driving WS2812B addressables and reacting to audio in real time. The layout went through a few passes to land on a two-layer board to keep cost down. Running daily on my desk and the inspiration for CAPSOUND.
